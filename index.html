with a purpose

Selvatico is a naturalistic cosmetic inspired by nature.
We are collaborating with the world's best french perfumers
of Robertet and with a french liquid soap artisan who inherits,
preserves and develops the traditional method of Marseille.
We strive to write a new history in our beauty routine
with simple and effective products for the skin.
